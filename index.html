01 / About

Research across learning, control, and deployment.

My work spans quadruped loco-manipulation, autonomous aerial systems, robot perception, multi-agent coordination, and sim-to-real transfer.

I am an M.S. student in Cyberspace Security at Beihang University. I am interested in autonomous systems that must operate under physical, sensing, communication, and safety constraints—not only in isolated algorithms, but as complete and reproducible robotic stacks.

我是北京航空航天大学网络空间安全专业硕士研究生。我的研究聚焦具身智能与自主系统，尤其关注机器人在真实物理约束、感知误差、通信条件和安全边界下的可部署性，并通过完整系统验证将算法连接到真实任务。

B2+R5
Python · Isaac Sim Active

B2Arx_Isaac_Sim

A reproducible Isaac Sim workspace for a B2 quadruped with an ARX R5 arm. It integrates the official ZED X simulation stream, Isaac ROS Nvblox, Nav2, and a replaceable reinforcement-learning locomotion policy into a verified perception-to-joint-control pipeline.

面向 B2 四足机器人与 ARX R5 机械臂的一体化 Isaac Sim 工作区。项目将官方 ZED X 仿真流、Isaac ROS Nvblox、Nav2 与可替换的强化学习运动策略连接起来，完成从感知、建图、导航指令到关节控制的端到端验证。

isaac_ros_manipulation_arx_r5a

A patch-free ROS 2 overlay for physical ARX R5A pick-and-place. It connects RealSense and AprilTag perception to NVIDIA's behavior tree, Nvblox ESDF, cuMotion, and MoveIt while keeping the upstream Isaac ROS repository unchanged.

面向真实 ARX R5A 抓取任务的无补丁 ROS 2 Overlay。它将 RealSense、AprilTag 感知与 NVIDIA 官方行为树、Nvblox ESDF、cuMotion 和 MoveIt 连接起来，同时保持上游 Isaac ROS 仓库不被修改。

Isaac_Ros_CuMotion_ArxR5a

A community integration for controlling a physical ARX R5A through its vendor CAN driver with ros2_control, MoveIt 2, RViz, and NVIDIA Isaac ROS cuMotion. It provides the robot model, hardware adapter, and planning stack.

Python · Isaac Sim 5.1 Verified

arx-r5-isaac-sim

A standalone ARX R5A simulation that closes the loop from GPU motion planning through MoveIt and ros2_control to PhysX execution and ROS 2 state feedback. Arm and gripper actions, mimic joints, and status feedback are validated end to end.

独立的 ARX R5A Isaac Sim 仿真项目，打通 cuMotion GPU 规划、MoveIt、ros2_control、PhysX 执行与 ROS 2 状态回传闭环，并完成机械臂与夹爪 Action、mimic 关节和状态反馈的端到端验证。
